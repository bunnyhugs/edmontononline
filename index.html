Bridging the digital divide with affordable web services.

Edmonton Online is dedicated to increasing inclusivity and accessibility in technology. We provide individuals, small businesses, and non-profits with the tools, hosting, and education needed to thrive online.

Our Mandate

Accessibility, Inclusivity, and Agency.

Our core mandate is to ensure essential web services—including email, website hosting, end-to-end encryption, and web development—are accessible to everyone, regardless of their background or financial situation.

"We work to increase understanding, digital capacity, and self-reliance through hands-on training and ongoing mentorship."

Whether you are looking for a reliable, privacy-focused home for your project, or you want to learn the ropes to self-host and manage your own digital infrastructure, we are here to support you.

What We Offer

Services & Digital Education

We offer direct access to secure technical resources, alongside the knowledge to help you maintain them independently.

Managed Web Services

Free and low-cost provisioning for essential tools: reliable custom email accounts, high-performance web hosting, secure data storage, and end-to-end encryption setup.

Web Development

Guidance and foundational assistance for creating web projects. We help individuals and community organizations establish a clean, functional online presence.

Secure Browsing & Privacy

Tools and configurations designed to safeguard your privacy online. Learn how to protect your personal identity, manage encryption, and navigate the internet safely.

Education & Self-Hosting

Instruction, structured learning, and guidance for individuals who want to deploy, manage, and configure their own web services and infrastructures from scratch.

Join Our Community

Accessible Membership for Everyone

Our community brings together people of all technical backgrounds. Membership is designed to ensure no one is turned away due to financial barriers.

Who Can Join?

  • Private Individuals looking to learn or secure their footprint.
  • Small Businesses seeking affordable entry-level solutions.
  • Small Non-Profit Organizations optimizing tight budgets.
