EO Edmonton Online
Non-profit society

Affordable web services, education, and support for individuals and small organizations.

Edmonton Online helps members access free and low-cost email, hosting, secure browsing, web development, training, and more. Our goal is to increase accessibility and inclusivity in technology and the internet.

What we offer

Free and low-cost web services, including email, hosting, secure browsing, end-to-end encryption, and web development. We also provide guidance for members who want to manage their own services.

Who can join

Private individuals, small businesses, and small non-profit organizations are welcome. We prioritize accessibility so everyone can participate regardless of background or situation.

Membership model

Membership is pay-what-you-can, including $0. We suggest a contribution of $20 per year, and applications are reviewed by our board of directors.

Our mandate

We provide affordable web services to members and work to ensure technology is accessible. Our mandate includes making email, hosting, encryption, and web development available to all while building understanding and capability through education.

  • Accessible web services for individuals and small organizations
  • Support for self-managed services and technical education
  • Inclusive technology access for all backgrounds and situations
  • Community-focused, affordable membership

Join Edmonton Online

To become a member, complete a member application form. Our board of directors will review each application and aim to make web services available to those who need them.
